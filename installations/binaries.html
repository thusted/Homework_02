Binaries is an interactive painting, using capacitive touch sensors that trigger sound. With juxtaposing sources used for the audio samples (all pulled from nature) as well as opposing color schemes, Tori Pope and Tiana Husted hoped to create an immersive experience that had an elastic feel. The artists wanted to challenge traditional paradigms of visual art by encouraging participants to not merely look, but also touch the work.
