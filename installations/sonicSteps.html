Sonic Steps

An interactive sound garden featuring custom-built force sensitive resistors on recycled tires.

Previous Next

Sonic Steps is a sound-based installation where participants were welcomed to walk across a grid of stepping stones, each triggering a different sound. As more would people join, they beame a part of a collective, improvisatory composition, combining different sonic textures through movement.
